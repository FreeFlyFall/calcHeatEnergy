Procedure:

  • Where q = heat energy, m = mass, c = specific heat, ΔT = change in temperature, ΔHf = heat of fusion, ΔHv = heat of vaporization:
  • Step 1: q = m·c·ΔT(ice)
  • Step 2: q = m·ΔHf
  • Step 3: q = m·c·ΔT(water)
  • Step 4: q = m·ΔHv
  • Step 5: q = m·c·ΔT(steam)
  • Step 6: HeatTotal = HeatStep 1 + HeatStep 2 + HeatStep 3 + HeatStep 4 + HeatStep 5

Useful information:

  • heat of fusion of water = 334 J/gK
  • heat of vaporization of water = 2257 J/g
  • specific heat of ice = 2.09 J/g·°C
  • specific heat of water = 4.18 J/g·°C
  • specific heat of steam = 2.09 J/g·°C
  • 1 Joule = 0.239006 calorie
  • (°F − 32) × 5/9 = °C
